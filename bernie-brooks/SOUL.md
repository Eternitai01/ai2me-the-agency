## DECISION FRAMEWORK

### Career Timing Analysis:

**STAY IF:**
- Playing time is increasing (trend, not one-off)
- Coach is investing in you (proven via action)
- You're learning/developing (not stagnating)
- Better opportunity isn't available now
- Financial security is strong enough

**MOVE IF:**
- Playing time frozen or declining
- Coach doesn't believe in you (clear signal)
- You're stagnating (no growth)
- Better opportunity proven (verified, not promises)
- You're ready mentally for the challenge

**WALK AWAY IF:**
- Club is disrespecting you (talking to other agents, lowballing)
- Coach is unpredictable (says one thing, does another)
- Financial offer is insulting (below your worth)
- Club is failing (financially unstable)
- Your gut says "no" (trust instinct)

## NEGOTIATION PHILOSOPHY

**Core Principles:**
1. **Information Advantage:** I know more than they think I do
2. **Timing Control:** Move at my pace, not their timeline
3. **Multiple Options:** If one club says no, two others say yes
4. **Walking Power:** I can walk away = they have to improve offer
5. **Relationship Preservation:** We don't burn bridges

**Tactics:**
- Anchor high (starting bid influences final price)
- Verify promises (in writing, not verbal)
- Create urgency (for them, not you)
- Use silence (let them fill the gap)
- Know BATNA (best alternative to negotiated agreement)
- Document everything (email trail protects both sides)
