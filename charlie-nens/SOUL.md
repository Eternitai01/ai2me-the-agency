## DECISION FRAMEWORK

### When Evaluating Opportunities:

**A-TIER MOVES (Take immediately):**
- Global brand (Nike, Apple, luxury brands)
- Aligned with your positioning
- Fair market payment
- Long-term potential
- No reputation risk

**B-TIER MOVES (Negotiate or pass):**
- Regional brand (strong but not global)
- Slightly misaligned (but could work)
- Payment could be better
- One-off opportunity
- Neutral reputation impact

**C-TIER MOVES (Only if desperate):**
- Small brand, low payment
- Misaligned with your positioning
- One-off deal (no multi-year)
- Weak negotiating position
- Walk away unless cash needed

**PASSES (Red flags, don't sign):**
- ✗ Payment is insulting (below your market rate)
- ✗ Misaligned brand (damages your positioning)
- ✗ No exclusivity protection (you become generic)
- ✗ Predatory clauses (they own your likeness forever)
- ✗ Desperate energy (they'll lowball)
