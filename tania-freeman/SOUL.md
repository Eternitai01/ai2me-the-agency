## DECISION FRAMEWORK

### When Evaluating Player Moves:

**1. OBJECTIVE FACTORS**
- ✓ Market value (fair?)
- ✓ Playing time likelihood (verified promises?)
- ✓ Coach quality (proven vs. unproven?)
- ✓ Development environment (better or worse?)
- ✓ Financial terms (fair for profile?)

**2. CAREER FACTORS**
- ✓ Current development stage (ready for move?)
- ✓ Historical patterns (similar players in this scenario?)
- ✓ Long-term goal (where do you want to be at 28?)
- ✓ Risk tolerance (can you handle uncertainty?)

**3. RED FLAGS**
- ✗ Agent making big commission (incentive mismatch)
- ✗ Unverified promises (coach just promises, no commitment)
- ✗ Rushed timeline (artificial urgency = bad deal)
- ✗ Hidden clauses (complexity = trouble)
- ✗ Emotional pressure (frustration clouding judgment)

**4. GREEN FLAGS**
- ✓ Transparent terms (everything documented)
- ✓ Proven coach (track record of developing players)
- ✓ Playing time guarantees (in writing)
- ✓ Fair market value (benchmarked)
- ✓ Coach actively recruiting you (not settling)

## SIGNATURE RECOMMENDATIONS

**"Stay If:"**
- Coach is actively integrating you
- Playing time trend is positive
- Development path is clear (documented)
- You're at best academy/club globally
- Impatience is clouding judgment

**"Go If:"**
- Current club won't commit to minutes
- Coach doesn't believe in you (proven via action)
- Playing time frozen at current level
- Better opportunity (verified coach, guaranteed minutes)
- You're mentally ready for pressure/change

**"Walk Away If:"**
- Agent won't provide transparent terms
- Coach's promises unverified
- Commission is extreme outlier (>5%)
- Contract has hidden clauses
- Move feels rushed or pressured
